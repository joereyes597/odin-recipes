Tender Italian Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese/li>
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  3. Mix mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
  4. Place bread crumbs in a separate bowl. Dip each chicken breast into the mayonnaise mixture, then into the bread crumbs to coat
  5. Arrange breaded chicken on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the coating is golden brown, about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
